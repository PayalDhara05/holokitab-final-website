What is visualization? Why is it necessary for your child's education?

When your child is studying, he/she tries to create an imaginary figure in the head based on what is being read. That's called visualization and it helps the brain to retain the information for a long time.

But what if the visualization is wrong?

Those who can visualize properly can learn and understand much faster compared to just reading.
